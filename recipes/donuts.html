Donuts

The only Donuts recipe you'll ever need

This recipe consists of a two-day process resulting in the best Donuts you'll ever taste!

*Using a mixer is optional but recommended*

Approximate Donuts you will get: 24-30

Ingredients:

  • 1kg All-Purpose Flour
  • 3 Eggs
  • 400g Whole Milk
  • 140g Sugar
  • 6g Salt
  • 110g Room-temperature Butter
  • 25g Fresh Yeast or 8g Dry Yeast

Classic glaze

  • 1kg Sugar
  • 100g Water

Chocolate ganache

  • 500g Bittersweet Chocolate
  • 500g Cream

Recipe instructions

  1. Mix the sugar with the milk.
  2. In a bowl mix the flour, milk and yeast. After the yeast is dissolved add the salt and eggs.
  3. After your dough becomes one piece take it out of the bowl and mix the butter.
  4. If you're not using a mixer the dough might be really sticky at first but don't panic. I recommend using the Bertinet kneading method. Keep kneading until all the butter has fully integrated into the dough.
  5. Your dough should start looking like a soft ball at this point. Put it in a container/bowl and put a blanket on top of the container.
  6. Let the dough rest in the fridge for 24 hours.
  7. Next day your dough should be double the size it was yesterday!. Carefully take it out of the container(don't let the air inside of the dough out!).
  8. With your hands push the air to the sides of the dough. Use a rolling pin to roll the dough into a large circle.
  9. Using a Donut mold start cutting and putting the donuts in a place where they can rest for another hour at least. With the remaining dough you can make more donuts so don't throw it.
  10. After one hour your Donuts should be ready to jump into your preferred cooking oil (I use animal fat). The oil should be at 160Â°C.
  11. They're ready to eat now!

    Optional glaze/ganache

  1. Mix the sugar with the water. That's it you're done!
  2. Heat the cream and pour it on top of the chocolate. Mix and voila!
  3. Now dip your delicious donuts, you earned it!
